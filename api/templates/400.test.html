USAGE

Retrieve points and uv data between a set of latitudes and longitudes at a given time and resolution.
https://hfrnet.ucsd.edu/map
Arguments
lat1 - northern boundary. default: 73
lon1 - western boundary. default: -170
lat2 - southern boundary. default: 13
lon2 - eastern boundary. default: -49
prod - combination of rtv product: hourly (h), 25hr average (a), month average (am), year average (ay) and resolution: 500m, 1km, 2km, 6km. default: a_6km
time - epoch time in seconds. default: 15 hours ago
callback - json callback function. default: eqfeed_callback

Example
https://hfrnet.ucsd.edu/map/?callback=jQuery341011901078419032718_1656599892559&lat1=40.12981741681639&lat2=34.08139909392745&lon1=-125.194091796875&lon2=-115.328369140625&time=1656540000&prod=a_6km
Retrieve a history of uv data at one latitude/longitude and resolution between a set of times
https://hfrnet.ucsd.edu/map/hist
Arguments
lat1 - northern boundary. default: 73
lon1 - western boundary. default: -170
prod - combination of rtv product: hourly (h), 25hr average (a), month average (am), year average (ay) and resolution: 500m, 1km, 2km, 6km. default: a_6km
t0 - epoch time in seconds. default: for hourly and 25hr average, 3 days before t1; for month average, 12 months before t1; for year average, 4 years before t1
t1 - epoch time in seconds. default: 15 hours ago
callback - json callback function. default: eqfeed_callback

Example
https://hfrnet.ucsd.edu/map/hist?callback=eqfeed_callback&lat1=34.619141&lon1=-122.989082&time=1656540000&prod=a_6km
